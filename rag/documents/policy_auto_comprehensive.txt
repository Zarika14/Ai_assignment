1. COVERAGE SUMMARY

This policy provides protection against various automobile-related risks as detailed below. The policyholder has selected comprehensive and collision coverage in addition to required liability protection.

2. LIABILITY COVERAGE

Bodily Injury per Person: $100,000
Bodily Injury per Accident: $300,000
Property Damage: $100,000

Liability coverage pays for injuries or property damage you are legally responsible for causing to others. This does not cover your own injuries or property.

3. COLLISION COVERAGE

Coverage Limit: Actual Cash Value
Deductible: $500
Deductible Waiver: Available for additional premium

Collision coverage pays for damage to your vehicle caused by collision with another vehicle or object, such as hitting a pole, guardrail, or another car. Regardless of fault determination, you pay the deductible and we pay the remaining repair or replacement cost up to the actual cash value of your vehicle.

4. COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE

Coverage Limit: Actual Cash Value
Deductible: $250
Special situations: Glass coverage available with $0 deductible

Comprehensive coverage pays for damage to your vehicle caused by events other than collision, including but not limited to: theft, vandalism, falling objects, weather events (hail, wind, flood), animal strikes, and fire. The deductible applies per claim.

5. UNINSURED/UNDERINSURED MOTORIST COVERAGE

Bodily Injury Limit: $100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident

This coverage protects you and passengers if struck by an uninsured or underinsured motorist. Coverage includes medical expenses and lost wages up to the specified limits.

6. MEDICAL PAYMENTS COVERAGE

Limit: $5,000 per person

Medical payments coverage pays for reasonable medical expenses incurred by you or your passengers resulting from an auto accident, regardless of fault.

7. EXCLUSIONS

This policy does not cover:
- Damage from racing, speed contests, or speed trials
- Intentional damage or destruction
- Damage while the vehicle is used for commercial purposes (rideshare, delivery services)
- Damage from wear and tear, mechanical breakdown, or faulty maintenance
- Loss due to violation of law or participation in illegal activities
- Damage while operating under the influence of alcohol or controlled substances
- Damage from nuclear hazard or war
- Damage from hired or borrowed vehicles (unless coverage extended)
- Damage occurring outside the continental United States without prior approval

8. CLAIMS PROCESS

To file a claim:
1. Contact our claims department within 72 hours of the incident at 1-800-CLAIM-99
2. Provide policy number, incident details, date, time, and location
3. Document all damage with photographs from multiple angles
4. Obtain police report for accidents involving other parties
5. Cooperate fully with the insurance company's investigation
6. Provide repair quotes from certified repair shops
7. Submit all required documentation within 30 days

Our claims adjuster will assess damage and determine repair versus replacement. For repairs under $2,500, you may use preferred repair shops with no deductible due to direct repair program. For larger claims, assessment may take 5-10 business days.

9. POLICY CANCELLATION

The policyholder may cancel at any time by calling customer service or submitting written notice. The company may cancel for non-payment after 10 days' notice, or for material misrepresentation after 30 days' notice.

10. RENEWAL

Your policy renews automatically 30 days before expiration unless cancelled. Renewal documents will be mailed 45 days before expiration. Premium may change based on driving record updates, claims history, and market conditions.

11. GRACE PERIOD

Payment is due by the due date shown on billing statements. A 10-day grace period applies for online payments; all payments must clear by the policy expiration date to avoid coverage lapse.
