1. PLAN OVERVIEW

This Bronze health insurance plan is designed for individuals and families seeking affordable coverage with moderate out-of-pocket costs. This plan covers preventive services at no cost and includes coverage for medical, prescription, dental, and vision services through an established provider network.

2. DEDUCTIBLES

Individual Annual Deductible: $3,000
Family Annual Deductible: $6,000
Embedded Deductible: Each family member has a separate deductible that does not exceed $3,000

The deductible is the amount you must pay out-of-pocket for covered services each plan year before the plan begins to share costs. After meeting your deductible, you pay copays or coinsurance for covered services.

3. OUT-OF-POCKET MAXIMUMS

Individual Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $7,000
Family Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $14,000

Once your out-of-pocket costs reach this limit during the plan year (including deductibles, copays, and coinsurance), the plan pays 100% of additional covered services for the remainder of that year.

4. COPAY STRUCTURE

Primary Care Physician Visit: $30 per visit
Specialist Visit: $60 per visit
Urgent Care Center: $75 per visit
Emergency Room: $300 per visit (waived if admitted to hospital)
Lab Work: $0 after deductible (preventive); $30 (diagnostic)
Imaging (X-ray, MRI, CT): $150 after deductible
Mental Health/Substance Abuse Therapy: $30 per session (up to 52 visits per year)
Physical/Occupational Therapy: $40 per visit (up to 30 visits per year)

5. PRESCRIPTION DRUG COVERAGE

Three-Tier Formulary:
- Tier 1 (Generic): $15 copay per 30-day supply
- Tier 2 (Preferred Brand): $40 copay per 30-day supply
- Tier 3 (Non-Preferred Brand): $75 copay per 30-day supply
- Specialty Drugs: 20% coinsurance after deductible (maximum $250 per prescription)

Maintenance medications (for chronic conditions) are covered at 3-month supplies with the following options:
- Mail-order pharmacy: $35 copay for 90-day supply
- Network retail pharmacy: $45 copay for 90-day supply

Prior authorization required for certain medications. Quantity limits apply to select medications per FDA guidelines.

6. PREVENTIVE SERVICES

Covered at 100% with no copay, coinsurance, or deductible when using in-network providers:
- Annual preventive care visits (wellness exam)
- Age-appropriate screenings (cancer, cardiovascular, metabolic)
- Vaccinations and immunizations
- Contraceptive methods (FDA-approved)
- Prenatal and postnatal care
- Depression screening
- Aspirin use screening
- Blood pressure screening
- Cholesterol screening

This includes only services classified as preventive under ACA guidelines. Diagnostic services, even if recommended during a preventive visit, may require a copay or coinsurance.

7. NETWORK REQUIREMENTS

This plan uses an Exclusive Provider Organization (EPO) network model. Coverage is significantly reduced for out-of-network services:
- In-Network: Copay/coinsurance applies
- Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance after $5,000 out-of-network deductible (separate from in-network deductible)

Using in-network providers is strongly encouraged to minimize costs. Search the provider directory at www.healthcareplus.com or call 1-800-HC-PLUS-1.

8. PRE-AUTHORIZATION REQUIREMENTS

Pre-authorization (approval before receiving service) is required for:
- Non-emergency hospitalizations
- Outpatient surgeries
- MRI and advanced imaging (CT, PET scans)
- Certain specialty services (sleep studies, etc.)
- Durable medical equipment
- Physical therapy sessions beyond initial 5 visits
- Organ transplants
- Experimental treatments

Failure to obtain pre-authorization may result in partial or full denial of coverage. Emergency services are generally covered without pre-authorization.

9. COVERED SERVICES

Medical Services:
- Hospital inpatient and outpatient services
- Physician visits and consultations
- Diagnostic testing and imaging
- Surgery and related procedures
- Mental health and behavioral health services
- Substance abuse treatment (inpatient and outpatient)
- Maternity and newborn care
- Rehabilitation services
- Home health care (when medically necessary)
- Skilled nursing facility (up to 60 days per year)

NOT Covered:
- Cosmetic procedures unless medically necessary
- Fertility treatments and artificial insemination
- Weight loss surgery and programs
- Experimental or investigational treatments (unless part of approved clinical trial)
- Routine vision care beyond annual eye exam

10. EXCLUSIONS AND LIMITATIONS

The plan does not cover:
- Services not medically necessary
- Services for non-covered conditions
- Elective procedures
- Alternative medicine and acupuncture (except certain approved therapies)
- Non-prescription medications
- Medical devices and supplies not on approved list
- Services received before plan effective date
- Services from non-network emergency facilities (except life-threatening emergencies)
- Charges exceeding reasonable and customary rates
- Self-inflicted injury or injuries from illegal activity

11. CLAIMS AND APPEALS PROCESS

Submit claims within 1 year of service date. Claims can be submitted:
- Online portal: www.healthcareplus.com/member
- Phone: 1-800-HC-PLUS-1
- Mail: HealthCare Plus Claims Department, P.O. Box 2847, Des Moines, IA 50306

Most in-network claims are processed within 15 business days. To appeal a denied claim, you have 180 days from denial date. Submit written appeal with documentation to the appeals department.

12. RENEWAL AND TERMINATION

This coverage renews annually on January 1 unless terminated. Coverage terminates if:
- Premium payments are 30 days past due
- You request cancellation with 30 days' notice
- You become eligible for Medicare or Medicaid
- You lose eligible group member status (employment termination)
- Group coverage is terminated by employer
